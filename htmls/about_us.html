ABOUT US


Founded in Argentina. The Green Closet is a contemporary clothing brand known for its trend-driven styles with affordable prices.

Drawing inspiration from the latest trends, from street style to runway.
The Green Closet clothing brand offers an array of styles that is fit for the fashion loving person. From workwear to street style.
